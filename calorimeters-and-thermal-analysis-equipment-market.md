<p><p>Calorimeters and thermal analysis equipment are used to measure various thermal properties of substances. There are two main types of analyzers in the market: single-functionality analyzers and simultaneous-functionality analyzers. </p><p>Single-functionality analyzers are designed to measure only one thermal property at a time, such as specific heat capacity or thermal conductivity. They are cost-effective and suitable for users who require measurement of a specific property.</p><p>Simultaneous-functionality analyzers, on the other hand, are capable of measuring multiple thermal properties simultaneously. This allows for a more comprehensive analysis of the sample and is preferred by users who need to evaluate various thermal characteristics in a single experiment.</p></p>

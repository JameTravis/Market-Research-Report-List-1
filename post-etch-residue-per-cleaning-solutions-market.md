They are commonly used in industries where complete removal of residues is critical and where water-based solutions may not be sufficient.</p><p>Both aqueous and semi-aqueous PER cleaning solutions play a crucial role in maintaining cleanliness and product quality in various manufacturing processes.</p></p>

<p><p>Two-wheeler transmission systems refer to the mechanism that transfers power from the engine to the wheels of a two-wheeler vehicle. The transmission systems play a crucial role in determining the performance, efficiency, and overall driving experience of two-wheelers.</p><p>The two-wheeler transmission systems market has been witnessing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, the increasing demand for two-wheelers across the globe, especially in developing countries, has been a major driver for the market. Rising urbanization, improving infrastructure, and affordable prices of two-wheelers have further fueled this demand. Secondly, technological advancements, such as the introduction of automatic and continuously variable transmission (CVT) systems, have enhanced the convenience and ease of use for riders.</p><p>The market is also witnessing a shift towards electric two-wheelers, driven by increasing environmental concerns and stricter emission regulations. Electric two-wheelers usually employ simpler transmission systems, resulting in reduced maintenance and lower costs.

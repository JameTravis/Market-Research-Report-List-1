The single-stage type combines injection molding and stretch blowing into one continuous process, allowing for faster production cycles. On the other hand, the two-stage type separates the injection molding and stretch blowing processes, enabling greater flexibility in bottle design and sizes. Both types cater to the growing demand for plastic packaging in various industries around the world.</p></p>

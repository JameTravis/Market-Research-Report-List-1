<p><p>Biotechnology separation systems market types include various methods such as membrane filtration, liquid chromatography, centrifuge, electrophoresis, flow cytometry, and others. Membrane filtration involves using a semi-permeable membrane to separate particles based on their size. Liquid chromatography separates biomolecules based on their interactions with a stationary phase. Centrifuge utilizes centrifugal force to separate substances based on their density. Electrophoresis separates molecules based on their charge and size. Flow cytometry analyzes and sorts cells based on their properties. Other methods include adsorption chromatography, precipitation, and size exclusion chromatography. These techniques play a crucial role in various biotechnological applications and research.</p></p>

<p><p>Near-infrared spectroscopy (NIRS) market includes two main types: FT-NIR (Fourier Transform Near-Infrared) and UV-Vis-NIR (Ultraviolet-Visible-Near Infrared). FT-NIR spectroscopy utilizes an interferometer and is widely used for high-resolution measurements in research and laboratory settings. UV-Vis-NIR spectroscopy, on the other hand, combines ultraviolet, visible, and near-infrared regions for a broader wavelength range, making it suitable for various applications such as pharmaceutical analysis, material science, and food quality control. Both market segments cater to different industries and offer unique advantages in terms of accuracy, sensitivity, and versatility.</p></p>

Ozone Sterilization: Machines equipped with ozone generators release ozone gas to kill bacteria and viruses present in the air.</p><p>4. Hypochlorous Acid Disinfection: These machines produce hypochlorous acid, a powerful disinfectant, to effectively eliminate pathogens in the air.</p><p>All these market types aim to provide clean and sterile air for improved indoor air quality and overall health.</p></p>

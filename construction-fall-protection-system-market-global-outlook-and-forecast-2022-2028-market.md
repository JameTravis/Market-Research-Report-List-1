These systems can be categorized into various types such as harness, lanyard, self-retracting lifeline, belt, and others. Harnesses are worn by workers and provide support and attachment points for other fall protection devices. Lanyards are safety ropes that connect the harness to an anchor point. Self-retracting lifelines automatically adjust the length of the lanyard. Belts provide additional support and stability. Other fall protection systems include nets, guardrails, and anchor points. This market is expected to grow globally from 2022 to 2028.</p></p>

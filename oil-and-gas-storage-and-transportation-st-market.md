<p><p>The Oil and Gas Storage and Transportation (S&T) market comprises various types of storage and transportation methods for oil and gas products. These include:</p><p>1. Pressurized Natural Gas (PNG): Natural gas compressed and stored at high pressure for transport and usage.</p><p>2. Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG): Natural gas cooled to a liquid state for easier storage and transportation.</p><p>3. Compressed Natural Gas (CNG): Natural gas compressed and stored at high pressure in special containers for transport and usage.</p><p>4. Adsorbed Natural Gas (ANG): Natural gas stored by adsorption onto materials, allowing for increased storage capacity.</p><p>5. Natural Gas Hydrates (NGH): Natural gas stored in solid ice-like form under high pressure and low temperature conditions.</p><p>6. Others: This represents other storage and transportation methods used in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>

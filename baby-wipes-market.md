<p><p>The market for baby wipes can be categorized into different types based on the manufacturing process used. Spunlace wipes are made using a spunlace technique, which creates a soft and strong fabric. Airlaid wipes are made using a process that combines fibers with air to form a absorbent material. Coform wipes are made by layering fibers with a binding material, resulting in a soft and bulky wipe. Needle punch wipes are created by punching needles through layers of fibers, producing a strong and durable wipe. These different types cater to varying preferences and needs of consumers in the baby wipes market.</p></p>
